## 🚀 Live Interactive Demo

We have hosted the entire WASI Receptionist Dashboard online so you can experience it live! Since our WhatsApp bot requires a persistent WebSocket connection, the backend orchestrator and Baileys WhatsApp adapter are running 24/7 on our local hardware server, bridged seamlessly to our Vercel-hosted frontend dashboard.

**How to try the demo:**
1. Open the Live Dashboard: **[wasi-dashboard.vercel.app](https://wasi-dashboard.vercel.app)**
2. Look at the bottom-left sidebar of the dashboard to find the **"SCAN TO ORDER"** QR code.
3. Open the camera app on your phone and scan the QR code.
4. It will open a WhatsApp chat with our live AI Receptionist! Say "Hi" or ask for the menu.
5. As you chat and place an order on your phone, watch the **Vercel Dashboard** update in real-time as the AI automatically logs your order!

*Note: If you wish to run the entire stack locally yourself, instructions are available in the repository.*
