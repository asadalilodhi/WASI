A multi-agent AI-powered food ordering platform delivered as a SaaS template for fast food restaurants. Customers place orders via WhatsApp, a pipeline of AI agents (Supervisor, Menu, Order, Delivery, Payment, Notification, Session, Language) processes the conversation and builds a validated order JSON, and receptionists manage incoming orders through a real-time dashboard with floating order cards, quick replies, and a one-click confirm workflow. Supports Urdu, English, and Sindhi. White-label ready — swap config, not code.
